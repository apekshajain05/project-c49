Interesting Facts about Apples

  • The science of apple growing is called pomology.
  • 25 percent of an apple’s volume is air; that’s why they float.
  • Apples are a member of the rose family of plants, along with pears, peaches, plums and cherries.
  • In 1730, the first apple nursery was opened in Flushing, New York.
  • Apple are one of the most widely grown tree fruit.

Interesting Facts about Doves

  • Doves are able to adapt and survive in just about any climate.
  • Doves are some of the strongest fliers of any bird species.
  • The term “dove” is rooted from a Germanic word that refers to their tendency to dive.
  • They are a symbol of peace and good luck in many cultures and religions.
  • Doves belong to the scientific family called Columbidae.

Interesting Facts about Goldfish

  • There are over 30000 known species of fish.
  • Although jellyfish and crayfish have the word ‘fish’ in their name, they aren’t actually fish.
  • Many fish can detect motion in the water using a special row of scales with sensors known as the lateral line.
  • They have been on Earth for more than 500 million years!
  • Fish make up about half of all known vertebrate species.

Interesting Facts about Swans

  • They are very large birds and can weigh up to 30 pounds
  • Swans are highly intelligent and sharp vision and impeccable hearing.
  • Swans live for approximately 20 to 30 years.
  • Mute swan, one of the heaviest flying birds, is a highly intelligent bird. If you have treated a swan kindly, it will remember you as a friend forever. It remembers its enemies as well.
  • Swans can fly as fast as 60 miles per hour!

Interesting facts about Turkeys

  • Turkeys are intelligent and sensitive animals that are highly social.
  • Wild turkeys have very powerful legs and can run at speeds up to 25 miles per hour. Their top speed in flight is 55 miles per hour.
  • They create lasting social bonds with each other and are very affectionate; rather similar to dogs.
  • Turkeys have the ability to learn the precise details of an area over 1,000 acres in size.
  • Turkeys have 5000 to 6000 feathers.

Interesting facts about Puppies

  • Puppies are born blind and deaf
  • They like when you sing-talk to them
  • Some puppies are born green.
  • Puppies know how to manipulate you with their eyes
  • They spend 15–20 hours a day sleeping.
